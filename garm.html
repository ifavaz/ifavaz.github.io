Founded on 15th of October 2003, EVE GRMENT is the pioneer and pacesetter of Eve Group of companies. Being a well renowned name nationwide, EVE GARMENT has been dedicated in designing, manufacturing and supplying a whole range of products associated with apparels, for the past 17 years, showing drastic growth, steady progress and remarkable improvement in its production and service delivery.

In order to cater for the growing demand and expansion in addition to the effective and efficient delivery of our services, an additional factory was set up in Thilafushi, in 2008, almost five years down the line as a leading Garment Production in the nation. This factory in Thilafushi was utilized for in-house large scale productions. Heading forward in the race of the usage of latest technology, machinery and resources for quality assurance, EVE Garment now uses cutting-edge technology and radical machineries which have earned us immense trust and reverence in the business and among all our clients.

Today, Eve Garment has expanded its services and is a renowned service provider not only in manufacturing garments, school uniforms and sportswear, but is also trusted for customizing upholstery, providing sports and school related accessories and for sourcing bulk fabric orders.

We excel in the production of uniforms as it is our most experienced product line. Although we first started off the production with the making of official school uniforms and sports uniforms, we are now the leading production partners for uniforms and official wear used in many of the well renowned organizations, companies, resorts and guesthouses in efficient operation. Our services in this area of expertise include designing uniforms and official attire to cater to the best of our capacity, the needs of the customers and to deliver the end product with its quality assured.

In enhancing our services, we not only offer our services to companies and organizations placing bulk orders, but we do cater the needs of individual customers too. Individual orders and orders made in bulk are both accepted with the complete assurance of the quality of the product never to be compromised in relevance to the quantity of the order received.

Further down the line of the services EVE Garments provide, designing, manufacturing and delivering the orders placed for custom made mascots are another ring to be added to the chain of services we offer, which are trusted and eminent among our customers.

Printing is yet another service we confidently provide and is one of our sturdiest areas in the services we provide. We upgrade the quality of our work in this area, by making sure the latest technology, machinery and devices are used in the process. We provide printing services on a wide range of products starting from uniforms, sportswear, garments, lanyard, proximity card, flasks, water bottles, pin badges, nametags, mugs, key chains and many more. To make our services the most attractive and customer oriented, for printing orders, we do provide complimentary designing services, to help our customers create the perfect designs on their preferred products.

Embroidery done on fabric adds to our line of services. We take bulk orders for embroidery done on woven patch with laser cut border or marrow border.

Custom made flags are also one of our specialties of production work which we are well known for. EVE Garment has earned the name for being the most reliable production partner in supplying tailor made national flags and flags of all designs with the highest quality of the product assured and provided.

Eve Garment being a trusted partner in all services the company provides, we have enhanced our services by establishing more companies under the EVE chain, specifically for the production of some of the products. 17 years of unwavering services provided for our customers, our greatest achievement is the trust we have gained from our every growing number of customers. Their satisfaction is our encouragement in raising our bars and for continuing to excel in the quality of the services we provide. We are ever so grateful for the recognition awards we have received for our services. International Star Award- Geneva 2005, Achiever of Industrial Excellence Award 2006, Srilanka, The National award won in 2007 in the category of Maldives Tailoring Industry and Special Merit Award by CNCI Achiever’s Award for Industrial Excellence under SAARC Countries 2019, Srilanka are some of the remarkable achievements we are proud of.

Our continuous efforts in striving for excellence have made us a well renowned leader in our field of work. Today we are proud to be the trusted partner in delivering our products to the largest population of public schools, companies and organizations across the nation.

Eve Garment Pvt Ltd is not just a name when it comes to the services we provide, we are a team of dedicated and devoted manufacturers and producers, who have made a significant difference for our clients with the best delivered with no compromises made in the quality of our services. With a heedful management and ardent and skillful experts in our team, we would continue to gratify the needs of our clients with much pride and satisfaction in the excellence of our work.
