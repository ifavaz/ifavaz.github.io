INTRODUCTION


Eve Garment Pvt Ltd, is the pioneer of Eve Group of Companies. We first began our production on October 15th, 2003. For the past 13 years, we have been dedicated in designing, manufacturing and supplying whole range of apparel related items.

In 2008, to expand our services in production, we introduced an additional factory in Thilafushi for the in-house large scale productions. Furthermore, to expand our Printing Unit, we now use cutting-edge printing technologies and machineries.



Today, we not only manufacture school uniform and sports wear, but a vast range including customizing upholstery, provide sports and school related accessories to sourcing bulk orders of fabric.

As a result of our continuous commitment towards excellence, we now supply sports uniform for more than 93% of the inhabited island schools, 80% of Male Schools and upholstery and related services for 11% of the hotels and resorts in Maldives.

Eve Garment Pvt Ltd has always been a team work driven by its excellent management team and effective workforce. Together we are visionary and we would continue to devote our services to our clients by providing the best at quality, using the latest technologies and by reinforcing social responsibility.
